No timer. No score. The reading takes as long as it takes.

How it works

  1. Pick a lens. Each lens asks you to notice different things.
  2. Click a phrase in the passage that the lens illuminates. The phrase will underline in the lens's colour. Click again to remove.
  3. Every third correct mark, write a one-or-two sentence justification — what does this phrase reveal under this lens?
  4. Switch lens when you want. The passage stays; the marks of other lenses dim. Each lens shows you something different.
  5. "Develop the photograph" when you're done with a passage. The expert reading is shown alongside your marks.

You cannot lose. False positives are part of close reading. The Reflection screen shows you both what you found and what you missed.

Keyboard: Tab to a phrase, Enter to mark or unmark. Tab to a lens, Enter to switch. Esc to pause.
